ABOUT

Cowater (born Fidelis Ogbe) is a rising star in the world of Afro-Fusion. With a dynamic blend of Afrobeat, Highlife, and contemporary influences, Cowater brings a fresh and electrifying energy to the music scene. Born on April 18, 1999, Cowater discovered his passion for music at a young age, drawn to its universal language of expression. His journey into music began as a form of personal exploration, seeking solace and inspiration through melodies and rhythms. Cowater's musical roots run deep, with his upbringing in the church choir playing a significant role in shaping his sound and style. The soul-stirring harmonies and infectious rhythms of gospel music infused his early musical experiences, laying the foundation for his expressive vocal delivery and emotive storytelling.

Although not directly inspired by his cultural heritage, Cowater's upbringing in Warri undoubtedly shaped his artistic identity, instilling in him a deep appreciation for diversity and a keen sense of resilience. His experiences growing up in a dynamic environment fueled his creativity and imbued his music with authenticity and depth. Driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence, Cowater has quickly gained recognition for his captivating performances and soulful lyrics. With each song, he invites listeners into his world, sharing stories of love, resilience, and the beauty of life. With a growing fan base and an unwavering dedication to his craft, he is poised to make a lasting impact on the global music scene. Keep an eye on Cowater as he sets the stage on fire and takes the world by storm with his infectious grooves and undeniable talent.
